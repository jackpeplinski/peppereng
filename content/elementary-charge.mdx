Elementary charge ($e$ or $q_e$) is the magnitude of charge carried by one electron or proton.


$$
|q_e|=|e|= 1.602176634×10^{-19} C
$$

$$
|q_e|=|e|≈1.6×10^{-19} C
$$

- All charges are integers of the elementary charge.
- The following particles charges are:

| Atomic Particle | Elementary Charge | Coulomb Charge (approx.) |
| --------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| Proton          | +1e               | $+1.6×10^{-19} C$        |
| Neutron         | 0e                | 0                        |
| Electron        | -1e               | $-1.6×10^{-19} C$        |
