- The following particles charges are:

<center>

| Atomic Particle | Elementary Charge | Coulomb Charge (approx.) |
| --------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| Proton          | $+1e$               | <InlineMath math="+1.6×10^{-19} \text{C}"/>        |
| Neutron         | $0e$                | $0$                        |
| Electron        | $-1e$               | <InlineMath math="-1.6×10^{-19} \text{C}"/>        |
